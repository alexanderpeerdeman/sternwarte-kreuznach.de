Widefield Heart & Soul + h&χ Perseii

Objektliste:
IC 1805, IC 1848, NGC 869, NGC 884, Star Miram, NGC 952, IC 1851, NGC 896, NGC 1027, MK 6, Be 65, Cz 8, K 4, Cz 13, Tr 2, different dark nebulas
